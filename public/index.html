FTC Funeral Rule Compliance

The Funeral Rule requires you to provide a General Price List (GPL) to anyone who asks in person. Key requirements:

  • Provide GPL before discussing arrangements
  • Provide Casket Price List (CPL) and Outer Burial Container (OBC) Price List
  • Do not misrepresent legal/crematory requirements
  • Do not require casket purchase for direct cremation
  • Do not bundle required purchases
  • Use the Pricing & GPL section to manage your price lists
